THE CLARENCE

DEPARTMENT OF LIPIDS & ATP

We were formed after the Great Food Riots of 4251, where a burst of anarchy broke out after the monarchy forgetting to give any food or energy to their people while playing anti-gravity golf for three weeks.

The Miss City at the time, Miss City Petunia, proposed the idea for a government department to handle food and electricity. We view energy as anything needed to sustain life.

We listen to the absolute Monarchy.

Therefore citizens cannot contribute ideas, however they can apply for positions within the department.

FOOD DIVISION

We build & regulate the agriculture system.

The food system begins in our "Producing Towers".

These giant structures house a largescale spiral staircase in the center of the fruit and vegatable gardens.

Each tower employs over 300 people that maintain, cultivate and harvest the food. The public can collect their fresh produce from our food towers. The towers close to the beaches have a delivery system to get food to our fellow aquatic citizens of Clarence.

Pictured above is a model of the grocery structure. The first on the left shows the most popular areas for citizens, the middle shows the center spiral staircase, and the right shows how the largest trees are organized.

A Closer Look at the Food Towers

Depicted below is the central spiral staircase any citizen or employee may take to any of the produce floors.
If a citizen is unable to use the staircase, they may choose to have their food delivered to their home.

The uppermost floor in the towers are dedicated to harvesting food for the monarchy and the palace.

Any food waste will enter the sophisticated composting system and be used to support plant growth.

ELECTRICITY DIVISION

We supply power to the public, their homes and other buildings. As well as public light. Everywhere in the city of Clarence we have stretched our net of electricity nodes. They can supply energy to anything! Just connect a node to any building and by the physical connection of touch, any appliance and electronic inside the building can function.

CLOTHING DIVISION

We provide energy converting clothes for all citizens.
We look to biomimicry to capture the most energy as possible. Because plants rule us all and the people must become more plant like.

The designs come directly from the Clarence department of aesthetics and our engineers ensure the functionality as well as the comfort of the clothing.

All clothing wirelessly uploads collected energy to the electricity node web.
