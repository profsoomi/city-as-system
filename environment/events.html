Events

Clarence holds a variety of different events around the islands, there's always something to do and be engaged in. Any and all events that could possibly imagined are open to public participation and can be found listed here.

Strolling In the Royal Gardens

A monthly event in the royal gardens at Palace Island. Take this chance to view the beautiful scenery and experience the fresh air where our leaders take residence.

Discovering Wildlife

What kinds of things actually live on our Trash Island? Join the Wildlife Crew on their walk around the island and discover its special ecosystem!

Register Here

Sand Castle Competition

Join the competition to build the best and most creative sand structure for a chance to win 5 mushrooms, or to just have some fun! Hosted and judged by the mermaids, open to all ages and species.

Register Here

Mushroom Appreciation Festival

The Monarchy is giving an exclusive opportunity to visit Mushroom Island, their private garden. Select few are able to come and appreciate the mushrooms that populate the gardens.

Register Here

Inside Trash Processing

Ever interested in what actually happens to all our trash? Take a tour of the state of the art processing plant on the island and see how we keep Clarence so clean.

Register Here

Pumpkin Picking

It's finally pumpkin season at The Farm! Come take a look at the hard work of the farmers and pick a pumpkin to take home!

Register Here

Surfing Fun

The Sand Bars are known for their beaches, perfect for watersports! Bring your own board, or rent one on the island. Meet other surfing enthusiasts and have fun out in the water!

Register Here

Intermediate Underwater Basket Weaving

Basket weaving, but underwater. Experience in basket weaving and being underwater required.

Register Here

Coral Reefs and Photography

The beautiful coral reefs around The Sand Bars are a sight to behold, one of the 4 wonders of the world. Bring an underwater compatible camera and yourself to participate.

Lights Festival on Main St.

In collaboration with the Energy Department, Grey Island has put up a beautiful and vibrant lights display on Main Street. Come experience the wonders of our sustainable energy system.

Good Eats is a weekly tour of different food establishments around the Grey Island. This week in the Slate Distict we visit Amore Pizza, run by Mister Amore, pizza masterchef. Join us and experience authentic Pizza cuisine in an authentic Pizza restaurant.

The Birthday Celebration

Time to celebrate everyone's birthday! Bring cakes, deserts, and presents for all!

I Am Farmer

Experience a day in the life of a farmer or farmhand, helping out at The Farm. Meet the people who work to produce the food all people in the city rely on and learn about the work that goes into it all.

Touring Clarence Monument

The monumental statue of our sovereign Clarence stands tall at the head of Palace Island and carries the long history of our city and world. Visit it in person to experience the magnitude and learn more.
