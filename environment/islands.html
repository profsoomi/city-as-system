Islands

Palace Island

A lush forestland, Palace Island is where the monarchy takes residence and where all the important decisions are made. The island itself is in the shape of our benevolent ruler's mortal figure, and the great Clarence statue stands at the island's peak.

Mushroom Island

Mushroom Island is the monarchy's private garden space, visitors are only allowed through special permission.

The Sand Bars

The Sand Bars are, as the name implies, sand bars. The golden sand and glistening waters are a perfect home to the mermaids that inhabit them. The underwater areas are out of reach to all except the mermaids, but the shore is a great place to watch the sunset.

The Farm

The Farm is a donut shaped island, but with a central township in place of the hole in the middle. This island is where a majority of the city's agricultural products are produced, thus giving the place its signature multicolor landscape.

Trash Island

Trash Island is where the city's waste is processed. The island currently looks like a fried egg due to the great job the processing center is doing, keeping the waste to a minimum: a majority of the island's space is unocupied by trash.

Grey Island

Grey Island is a residential sector, providing a clean, organized, and efficient mode of living. Most of the city's population resides here and finds their needs completely satisfied.
