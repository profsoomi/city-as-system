Manifesto:
We have a purpose, to spread our seed to every corner of the world. Long ago in the barren wasteland that was the world the first Clarence, Clarence the Uniter, appeared and with them our seed came. The seed made what we know now, mushroom filled valleys and mountains, the start of a new civilization named Clarence, in his honor. Through our mushroom filled existence we bring a great life for our people, we know what is best for our people. No one can stop the force that is Clarence and those we oppose will perish. We are a civilization of dreams and potential and we will direct the world into our vision that is greatness. Everybody will follow us and we’ll make sure of it because we know what is the correct path towards the perfect future. Mushrooms are a way of life. We live, we breathe and we consume mushrooms. They are everything to us and they should be everything to the rest of the world and believe firmly in that. We will stop at nothing to ensure everybody experiences life under our rule. Mushrooms are the elixir of life, the good to the bad, we as a community need to work together to keep it that way. Our life is devoted to its survival and yours should be too. As a people devoted we have to be devoted to our survival, to our expansion, to the spreading of our seed. Follow our rules and you’ll have a great life in Clarence. The monarchy knows the best for you, do no doubt it and definitely do not antagonize us. We are everything and we can help you be everything.
Clarence The Uniter
Clarence The Uniter came to us and was the bringer of the seed. They were the first and with their immense power united the rouge factions that ruled the land at the time. They ruled for 100 years until their death came and it was a sad day. No one will ever forget The Uniter as they are the reason we are here.
Clarence The Wise
They created a revolutionary serum out of mushrooms to help citizens unify fully under us. They were assassinated by a spy of an enemy state.
Clarence The Gray
The Gray was a gracious human, a fair ruler and a lover of science. There was peace during his rule, harmony.
Clarence The Victorious
Long ago, during the reign of The Gray, our community lived together in harmony. Then, everything changed when the angry mushrooms attacked. Only Clarence, master of all mushrooms, could stop them, but when the world needed him most, he vanished. A hundred years passed and my brother and I discovered the new Clarence. And although his skills are great, he has a lot to learn before he's ready to save anyone. But I believe Clarence can save the world
Clarence The Absolute
They rule with an iron fist. There are no mistakes they make as they know everything. Their word is final and is the correct one.
